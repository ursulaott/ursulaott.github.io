notes from a dead house

01.27.2021

This week I read Notes from a Dead House by Fyodor Dostoevsky. The semi-autobiographical work details the experience of prisoners in a Siberian labor camp. My professor called it "the orange is the new black of the 1860s" which I found to be quite apt. The work is truly a study of what it means to be human and civilized, and the astonishing ability of man to adapt to any circumstance.

I rate No Longer Human 4 out of 5 stars.
