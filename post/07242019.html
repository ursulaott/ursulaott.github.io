goals

09.24.2019

I have tried many methods of setting goals in the past. These have ranged from daily goals in a bullet journal, weekly goals, monthly, yearly, you name it.

After a few years of trial and error, to be honest I'm still not sure what the best method of goal-setting is. However, this time I am going to try a new experiment: goals for the semester.

As a college student, my life revolves around the timeline of a semester. It should only be fitting that my personal goals should follow along with this structure.

It's still July, and the semester doesn't start for over a month, but I still want to start planning so I make sure my goals are realistic and achievable.

I have started a list of things that I want to achieve by the end of the semester, aka December 2019:

  • Work, and save/invest $2,000
  • Minimize what I own by 20%
  • Establish a consistent workout schedule, with lifting
  • Secure leads for a job next summer
  • Maintain my same GPA or higher
