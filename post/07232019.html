everything i own

07.23.2019

So, like most other obnoxious minimalists, I take some amount of pride in owning a low number of physical objects. Or, to be honest, I did. Over time, I inevitably accumulate more "things", and I have to periodically declutter. Two of the hardest categories for me are clothing and books.

I decided it would help in my decluttering to take an inventory of all of my belongings, for 2 reasons. First, just to keep track in a objective sense. Second, I believe that this process will help me realize I have too much, and help me in the decluttering process.

Things I have with me right now

  • Olive green hydroflask
  • iPhone 6s with case and charging cable
  • MacBook pro 13 inch with charging cable
  • Wallet
  • Self-care items (chapstick, hair ties, hairbrush, toothbrush)
  • Men's dress shoes
  • Black jeans
  • Black T shirt
  • Socks & undergarments
  • Grey Patagonia Fleece
That is already 10 items! (More if you count differently). These are all essentials, but I have a lot of things at home that I use much less. I also have things stored in California that I don't use at all right now, and a box of winter clothes and room decor somewhere in NYC that will be delivered to my dorm at the beginning of next semester.

The results of my inventory of all of my things will come in the next post. I made a list, but I'm not sure I'll share it just yet. For sure, my goal is to get rid of at least 20% of what I own right now before I leave back to California.
