crime and punishment

01.13.2021

First week's book finished a little late, on the 11th. Dostoevsky's Crime and Punishment was a very interesting way to start the year. I consumed it largely as an audiobook, with some e-reading here and there. This book inspired me to sign up for a course on russian literature this semester; I am looking forward to reading more of Dostoevsky's work later on this year.

I rate Crime and Punishment 5 out of 5 stars.
