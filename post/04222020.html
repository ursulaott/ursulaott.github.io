things i love

04.22.2020

Now that I have so much free time I've been able to get back in touch with what I love. I'm writing this because I want to remember what made me happy in these strange quarantine days.

  • taking a walk for 2 or 3 hours to main street cupertino or just around the neighborhood
  • listening to masters of scale during my walks
  • calling a friend to talk about random ideas
  • mastering a tricky recipe (croissants)
  • drinking a mint mojito from philz, when i can get there before they close super early
  • looking at all of the plants around the apple campus
  • looking at videos and pictures of matcha the baby turtle

Honestly walking for 2 hours a day is changing my life. I don't have much else to do, and it's making my anxiety so much less prevalent in my thought patterns. I don't think it's sustainable to keep up the habit during school but I will for sure try to incorporate it into my routine now that I feel the benefits.
