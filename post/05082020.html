big beliefs

05.08.2020

In order to grow and develop as a person, beliefs must change over time as new evidence is introduced. I thought it would be a fun exercise to evaluate my beliefs about certain big topics, and revisit them later to see how I've changed. I'm taking this list as a reference guide, but also am going to add a few extras on top. There is a much more extensive list on Wikipedia for the curious. Side note⁠—I find it extremely strange that a person existing in and of themselves can be controversial, but I guess that's just anoter belief I can add to the list.

I listed everything out here, and I'm setting a reminder to re-evaluate all of these on this day in 2021.

I think my strongest beliefs at this point have to do with technology, climate change, and mental health, because these are the things that affect me the most on a personal level at this time in my life. I'm still unsure about a lot of the "controversial topics" I went through, so I'm going to spend some time researching them more.
