distance formula

09.09.2019

I know it's not too pc to say this, but I am "pretty OCD" about a lot of things. I scored very very high in neuroticism in the big 5 personality index (also high in disagreeableness... go figure). But overall I am not a chill person and my anxiety overcomes my better judgement 99% of the time, much to the annoyance of anyone that's close to me.

Some people that are close to me have even remarked that my tendencies make them frustrated and wish that I would stop.

Because of this, I decided that I am going to create distance. I figured, that if I spread out my time over a lot of different activities and fill my days with many obligations, I can create distance. No one person has to deal with me very often, and I will be more focused on getting things done than worrying or overthinking.

More things to do = seeing any one individual person less = less thinking, more doing.

Or at least, I hope it works out like that.

this might sound a little depressing, but honestlty it's the best i've got at this point
